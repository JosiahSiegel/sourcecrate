Why Open Source?

Academic research tools should be accessible to everyone — especially researchers without institutional funding. SourceCrate is:

  • Privacy-first: 100% client-side, no tracking
  • Transparent: All code is open source
  • Community-driven: Built by researchers, for researchers
  • Free forever: Sustained by voluntary support
